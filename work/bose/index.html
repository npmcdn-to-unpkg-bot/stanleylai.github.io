--- layout: work title: Bose Australia & New Zealand ---

Bose are specialists in audio equipment, renowned among travellers in particular for their noise-cancelling headphones.

Bose Australia & New Zealand were developing a new marketing strategy targeting a younger, trendier demographic without alienating their traditional audience.

They needed a mobile storefront to complement their efforts, and our team at Mobify worked with their Global Marketing team to make this happen.

My Role

I was the lead Interaction Designer on the project, leading the design process as well as writing the production front-end UI markup.

We were a small team of Designers, Project Managers, Front-end Engineers and QA Analysts working on a tight deadline.

Key Challenges

Refining the Brand for Digital & Mobile

Bose has a strong global brand standard for their print, physical and retail applications. They wanted us to help develop their digital standards.

Short Timeline

The project had to be delivered in time for their campaign launch, so we had a tight timeline to work within.

First Steps

Content Audit

We started out with an evaluation of the types of content that are key to the customer experience, then worked with the client team to organize and prioritize them.

Heuristic Analysis

We evaluated their current desktop experience and collaborated on what the priorities needed to be for mobile – along the way incorporating findings from their own research. As the project went along, we checked ourselves against those heuristics as a standard.

Design Focus

Product Listing page

Bose has a small product line-up, and this allowed us to be more verbose and deliberate when approaching how each product was presented. We ensured that the distinguishing elements of each product were clear and that a potential customer could make an informed decision simply and efficiently.

Product Comparison Table

There are small but important differences between many of Bose’s products. We created a mobile comparison feature to identify and highlight these for greater ease and efficiency in the decision-making process.

Checkout Flow

Bose had a great desktop checkout experience that we improved upon on mobile, cutting out fields, pages, and flows where possible.

Outcome

The client team loved our product and in fact took it a step further, rolling it out in other regions in their global network.

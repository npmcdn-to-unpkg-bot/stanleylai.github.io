--- layout: work title: Bose Australia & New Zealand ---

Bose are specialists in audio equipment, renown among travellers in particular for their noise-cancelling headphones.

Bose Australia & New Zealand were developing a new marketing strategy targeting a younger, trendier demographic without alientating their traditional audience.

They needed a mobile storefront to complement their efforts, and our team at Mobify worked with their Global Marketing team to make this happen.

My Role

I was the lead Interaction Designer on the project, leading the design process as well as writing the production front-end UI markup.

We were a small team of Designers, Project Managers, Front-end Engineers and QA Analysts working on a tight deadline.

Key Challenges

Refining the Brand for Digital & Mobile

Bose has a strong global brand standard for their print, physical and retail applications. They wanted us to help develop their digital standards.

Short Timeline

We had a tight timeline to deliver this project in time for their campaign launch

First Steps

Content Audit

We started out with an evaluation of the types of content that are key to the customer experience, and working with the client team to organize and prioritize them.

Heuristic Analysis

We evaluated their current desktop experience, and collaborated on what the priorities needed to be for mobile – along the way incorporating findings from their own research. As the project went along, we checked against those heuristics as a standard.

Design Focus

Product Listing page

Bose has a small product line-up, which allows us to be more verbose and deliberate how each product is presented. We ensured that the distinguishing elmeents of each is clear, and supported teh customer's decision making process.

Product Comparison Table

There are small, but important differences between each of their products. We created a mobile comparison feature to make that easier on mobile.

Checkout Flow

They had a great desktop checkout experience, that we improved upon on mobile – cutting out fields, pages and flows where possible.

Outcome

The client team loved what we came up with. Enough to begin rolling out the experience further to other regions in their global network.
